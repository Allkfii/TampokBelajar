🔵 Tipe A  —  Verb Errors ▼

1. Subject-Verb Agreement
Subjek tunggal (singular) harus diikuti kata kerja tunggal, dan subjek jamak (plural) diikuti kata kerja jamak.

✗ Salah: People has taken the free food.
✓ Benar: People have taken the free food.

2. Verb Form
Kesalahan umum: memakai past tense biasa padahal seharusnya past participle (untuk passive/perfect tense).

✗ Salah: This book was wrote by Mr. Joe.
✓ Benar: This book was written by Mr. Joe.
🔵 Tipe B  —  Word Form Errors ▼

1. Bentuk kata yang salah (noun/verb/adjective/adverb)

✗ Salah: I will mention three different between the books.
✓ Benar: I will mention three differences between the books.

2. Cardinal vs. Ordinal Number
Cardinal = menghitung (one, two, three). Ordinal = urutan (first, second, third).

✗ Salah: For the four time this month, Paula got the wrong bus.
✓ Benar: For the fourth time this month, Paula got the wrong bus.
🔵 Tipe C  —  Word Choice Errors ▼

Kesalahan memilih kata yang mirip tapi berbeda makna/penggunaan. Contoh kata yang sering tertukar:

  • too / so / such
  • most / almost
  • ever / never
  • no / none / not
  • after / afterwards
✗ Salah: Almost students passed the exam.
✓ Benar: Most students passed the exam.
(Almost dipakai sebelum kata sifat/keterangan, bukan sebelum kata benda)
🔵 Tipe D  —  Preposition Errors ▼

1. Preposisi yang salah

✗ Salah: We are not satisfied in the result.
✓ Benar: We are not satisfied with the result.

2. Preposisi yang hilang (omitted)

✗ Salah: She did not reply the email.
✓ Benar: She did not reply to the email.
🔵 Tipe E  —  Singular & Plural Noun Errors ▼

1. Each/every + singular noun

✗ Salah: The book must be read by each students.
✓ Benar: The book must be read by each student.

2. Uncountable nouns tidak bisa di-pluralkan
Contoh: information, advice, furniture, luggage, equipment

✗ Salah: I found some informations about the case.
✓ Benar: I found some information about the case.

3. Compound noun phrase

✗ Salah: Mr. Poe needs two pictures frames.
✓ Benar: Mr. Poe needs two picture frames.

4. Angka + kata benda (hyphenated adjective)

✗ Salah: I took a ten-minutes break.
✓ Benar: I took a ten-minute break.
🔵 Tipe F  —  Word Order Errors ▼

Dua kata ditukar posisinya sehingga kalimat menjadi salah.

✗ Salah: I got a long extremely lecture from Mr. John.
✓ Benar: I got an extremely long lecture from Mr. John.

Keterangan (adverb) harus muncul sebelum kata sifat yang dimodifikasinya.

🔵 Tipe G  —  Conjunction Errors ▼

1. Correlative Conjunctions (pasangan kata hubung)

Either A or B Neither A nor B
Both A and B Not only A but also B
✗ Salah: I want either iced-tea and lemon juice.
✓ Benar: I want either iced-tea or lemon juice.

2. And vs. But

✗ Salah: She usually takes the bus, and today she drove her car.
✓ Benar: She usually takes the bus, but today she drove her car.
🔵 Tipe H  —  Pronoun Errors ▼

1. Pronoun tidak cocok dengan noun

✗ Salah: He left his book, but he found them the next day.
✓ Benar: He left his book, but he found it the next day.

2. Pronoun yang tidak perlu (unnecessary)

✗ Salah: Dona, my older sister, she is a bright student.
✓ Benar: Dona, my older sister, is a bright student.

3. Bentuk pronoun yang salah

✗ Salah: This award was given to he.
✓ Benar: This award was given to him.
🔵 Tipe I  —  Comparative & Superlative Errors ▼

1. Double superlative (salah berganda)

✗ Salah: July is the most coldest month of the year.
✓ Benar: July is the coldest month of the year.

2. As...as harus pakai bentuk dasar (positive form)

✗ Salah: This book is as bigger as my old one.
✓ Benar: This book is as big as my old one.

Tipe G Conjunction Errors Pasangan konjungsi salah: either…or, neither…nor either…and → either…or
Tipe H Pronoun Errors Pronoun tidak cocok, tidak perlu, atau bentuk salah given to he → given to him
Tipe I Comparative/Superlative Errors Double superlative, as...as + comparative (salah) most coldest → coldest
📌 Cara Cepat Ingat:
  • Uncountable nouns TIDAK bisa ditambah -s: information, advice, luggage, furniture
  • Each / every selalu diikuti kata benda tunggal
  • As...as selalu pakai bentuk dasar kata sifat (bukan comparative)
  • Most untuk kata benda, almost untuk kata sifat/keterangan/verba
  • Neither…nor, either…or, both…and — pasangannya tidak bisa ditukar!
